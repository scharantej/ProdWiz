What is an MVP?

An MVP (Minimum Viable Product) is a version of a product that has just enough features to be usable by early customers.

Benefits of an MVP

  • Reduced development time and costs
  • Early feedback from customers
  • Increased chances of success

Steps for Creating an MVP

  1. Define the problem you are solving
  2. Identify your target customers
  3. Build a list of essential features
  4. Develop and test your MVP
  5. Get feedback from customers and iterate
