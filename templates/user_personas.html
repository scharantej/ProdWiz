What are User Personas?

User personas are fictional representations of your target customers. They help you to understand their needs, goals, and pain points.

Importance of User Personas

  • Improve product design
  • Increase customer satisfaction
  • Reduce churn rate

Guidelines for Developing User Personas

  1. Interview real customers
  2. Create a profile for each persona
  3. Use the personas to guide your decisions
